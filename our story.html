Story

Get in touch and close to our natural world

CIRCU HAIRCARE gives you a full circulation care from your scalp to the hair end. We extract every drop of essential oil and plant essence from nature. The plant and flowers using for extracting are picked up by hands from farms.

We are the world’s first haircare company that promise to achieve this: treat the hair in the mildest way with our products and gives the hair enough nutrition for the best texture in the meantime.

Science

Begin to know something important about your hair

Our science includes foaming agent infuse and natural ingredients extract. We use the world’s mildest foaming agent: amino acid foaming agent. We promise no silicone or its derivatives for the fake texture of hair. Alternatively, we add the real nutrition and nourish your hair.
